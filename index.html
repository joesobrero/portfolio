About Me

Welcome!

My name is Joseph Sobrero and I am a user experience designer.

If I did not work, I would spend all my time rock climbing. I love climbing because it is one of a few sports that is as physically challenging as it is mentally challenging. Strength is not the only factor because every climb is like a puzzle. Most importantly, climbing requires you to focus on your weaknesses and battle fear as you hang from tiny holds high up on the wall.

Aside from climbing, I love discovering new music, taking photos, creating evocative art, practicing philosophy, and taking time to do absolutely nothing.
